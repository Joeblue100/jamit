About Jamit


Diversifying the World Of Social Audio


Jamit is the social audio platform focused on making it easier for the world to discover amazing stories by diverse voices all over the world. Building a community where everyone can speak their minds, connect and be heard. We work with creators from all over the world, allowing them celebrate their cultures by telling their stories with ease for the world to listen to and enjoy.

Why Jamit


We saw the need for a platform that will at all times showcase and celebrate the voices of people of color (and not just in a specific month of the year). A place where users can express themselves authentically and get their stories to everyone who would love to hear it and also give a new way for others to learn about other cultures. It our mission to bring the next generation of diverse storytellers to the social audio scene. - Founded by industry veterans who previously founded and built TruSpot, the first African music platform which contributed tremendously to the growth and discoverability of the Afrobeats music space. Now, we are bringing that passion and expertise into the spoken word space.
